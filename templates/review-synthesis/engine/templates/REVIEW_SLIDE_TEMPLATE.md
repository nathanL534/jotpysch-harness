# Review Slide Template

Each person's review deck should contain these slides:

## Slide 1: Title
- Person's name and role
- Review cycle: {{reviewCycle}}
- Team: {{teamName}}

## Slide 2: Summary
- 2-3 sentence overall summary
- Highlight top strength and top growth area

## Slide 3: Strengths
- 3-5 specific strengths with evidence from reviews
- Each strength should reference concrete examples

## Slide 4: Growth Areas
- 2-3 growth areas with constructive framing
- Include suggested actions where possible

## Slide 5: Peer Highlights
- Notable quotes or themes from peer feedback
- Anonymized — never attribute to specific reviewers

## Slide 6: Goals
- Suggested focus areas for next cycle
- Connect to growth areas identified above

## Styling
- Use brand.css for colors, fonts, layout
- Each slide is a `<section>` in the HTML output
- Keep text concise — slides, not essays
